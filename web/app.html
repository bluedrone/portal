Rx Renewal

Step 1 of 2: Choose prescriptions to renew

Your MyHealth Online request to have a new updated (renewal) prescription written will be sent to your Clinician's office. Please submit a request if you have 0 refills on a medication.

The prescription must be approved by your provider. Once approved, it will be sent to your pharmacy within 2 business days. Please contact your pharmacy prior to picking up your medication to be sure it is ready.

Important Note: Please do not submit a MyHealth Online Rx Renewal Request if you simply need a medication refilled by your pharmacy. Instead you must contact your pharmacy directly to have your prescription refilled.

Send Message

Send Your Doctor A Message

We will attempt to respond to your message within one business day, but delays may occur. The Family Portal should only be used for routine and non-urgent matters. Please do not send messages through the Family Portal that require urgent attention.

Messages will be screened by your physician's staff to determine an appropriate person to respond to your request; please consider this when communicating sensitive information within your message.

Please note: the communication you send or receive may become a part of your medical record.

Send a non-urgent message to your clinic.

Expect a response within 2 business days.
